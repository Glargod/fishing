Canadian Live-Action Classics

  • The Friendly Giant (1958–1985) - Gentle storytelling with Bob Homme, Rusty, and Jerome.
  • Mr. Dressup (1967–1996) - Ernie Coombs’ crafts and Tickle Trunk adventures.
  • Fred Penner’s Place (1985–1997) - Musical show with songs like “The Cat Came Back.”
  • The Raccoons (1985–1992) - Animated but story-driven, with Bert Raccoon and friends.
  • Sharon, Lois & Bram’s Elephant Show (1984–1988) - Musical trio with sing-alongs like “Skinnamarink.”
  • Chez Hélène (1959–1973) - French-teaching show with Hélène and Suzie the Mouse.
  • Degrassi Junior High / High (1987–1991) - Gritty teen drama tackling real issues.
  • You Can’t Do That on Television (1979–1990) - Sketch comedy with green slime and irreverent humor.
  • Danger Bay (1985–1990) - Marine biologist family adventures in Vancouver.
  • Kids in the Hall (1988–1995) - Absurd sketch comedy for older Gen Xers.

Canadian Cartoons

  • Inspector Gadget (1983–1986) - Bumbling cyborg detective with Penny and Brain.
  • The Care Bears (1985–1988) - Kindness-promoting bears in a colorful world.
  • Babar (1989–2000) - Elephant king’s adventures in Celesteville.
  • Beetlejuice (1989–1991) - Quirky animated take on the Tim Burton film.
  • The Adventures of Tintin (1991–1992) - Journalist Tintin’s global adventures with Snowy.
  • Rocket Robin Hood (1966–1969) - Futuristic Robin Hood with campy sci-fi vibes.
  • The Mighty Hercules (1963–1966) - Mythological hero’s adventures, popular in reruns.
  • Spider-Man (1967–1970) - Web-slinging superhero with iconic theme song.

Imported Cartoons

  • Scooby-Doo, Where Are You! (1969–1970, reruns) - Mystery-solving gang with humor and spookiness.
  • The Flintstones (1960–1966, reruns) - Prehistoric family comedy with modern humor.
  • The Smurfs (1981–1989) - Blue creatures in a magical forest.
  • He-Man and the Masters of the Universe (1983–1985) - Epic battles of He-Man vs. Skeletor.
  • She-Ra: Princess of Power (1985–1987) - Empowering adventures of He-Man’s sister.
  • Transformers (1984–1987) - Autobots vs. Decepticons with toy-driven action.
  • Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles (1987–1996) - Pizza-loving turtles fighting crime.
  • Fraggle Rock (1983–1987) - Jim Henson’s musical Fraggles, filmed in Canada.
  • The Jetsons (1962–1963, reruns) - Futuristic family comedy.
  • Looney Tunes (1930s–1980s, reruns) - Bugs Bunny and friends’ slapstick humor.
  • Thundercats (1985–1989) - Cat-like heroes led by Lion-O in epic adventures.
  • DuckTales (1987–1990) - Scrooge McDuck’s treasure-hunting with nephews.
  • Captain Planet and the Planeteers (1990–1996) - Teens saving the planet with environmental themes.
  • Muppet Babies (1984–1991) - Baby Muppets in imaginative adventures.
  • Rocky and Bullwinkle (1959–1964, reruns) - Witty moose and squirrel with “Fractured Fairy Tales.”
