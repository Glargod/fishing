Background and Context

Cannabis terpenes are aromatic compounds found in the trichomes of the cannabis plant, contributing to its distinctive smell, taste, and potentially its therapeutic effects. Research suggests they may work synergistically with cannabinoids like THC and CBD, a phenomenon known as the entourage effect. This section elaborates on the terpenes, their effects, and strains, drawing from reliable sources.

Terpene Details

  • Myrcene: The most common terpene in cannabis, known for calming (sedative) and pain-relieving (analgesic) properties, also relaxing and reduces inflammation (anti-inflammatory). Found in strains like Mango Kush, Skunk XL, White Widow, OG Kush, Northern Lights, Granddaddy Purple, Blue Cheese, and Purple Urkle.
  • Limonene: Recognized for reducing inflammation (anti-inflammatory) and fighting cell damage (antioxidant), also lifts mood (anti-depressant). Common in Sour Diesel, Super Lemon Haze, Durban Poison, Jack Herer, Lemon Skunk, Wedding Cake, Do-Si-Dos, and Trainwreck.
  • Linalool: Known for reducing inflammation (anti-inflammatory) and pain-relieving (analgesic) effects, also reduces anxiety (anxiolytic), calming (sedative), and fights bacteria (anti-microbial). Found in Amnesia Haze, Lavender, LA Confidential, Pink Kush, Master Kush, G-13, Chem Dawg, and Kosher Kush.
  • Caryophyllene: Notable for reducing inflammation (anti-inflammatory) and easing arthritis, also pain-relieving (analgesic) and reduces anxiety (anxiolytic). Found in Girl Scout Cookies, Sour Diesel, Super Silver Haze, Zkittles, Northern Lights, Bubba Kush, Gelato, and Chemdawg.
  • Alpha-pinene and Beta-pinene: Both reduce inflammation (anti-inflammatory) and help breathing (bronchodilator); Alpha-pinene improves memory and fights bacteria (anti-microbial), while Beta-pinene lifts mood (anti-depressant) and fights bacteria (anti-microbial). Common in Jack Herer, Blue Dream, Dutch Treat, and Haze Berry or OG Kush, plus Trainwreck, Strawberry Cough, Romulan, Island Sweet Skunk (Alpha-pinene) and Harlequin, Cannatonic, Critical Mass, AK-47 (Beta-pinene).
  • Alpha-bisabolol: Improves absorption and provides pain-relieving (analgesic) effects, also reduces inflammation (anti-inflammatory) and fights bacteria (anti-microbial). Found in Harle-Tsu, Pink Kush, OG Shark, ACDC, Headband, Master Kush, Dolato, and Banana Kush.
  • Eucalyptol: Relieves pain and fights bacteria (anti-bacterial), also reduces inflammation (anti-inflammatory) and clears airways (expectorant). Present in Super Lemon Haze, Girl Scout Cookies, Headband, AC/DC, Bubba Kush, Super Silver Haze, Lemon Kush, and White Fire OG.
  • Trans-nerolidol: Fights parasites (antiparasitic) and cell damage (antioxidant), also calming (sedative) and fights fungus (anti-fungal). Found in Sweet Skunk, Island Sweet Skunk, Skywalker OG, Blue Dream, Jack Herer, Sour Diesel, Green Crack, and Black Jack.
  • Humulene: Slows cancer growth (anti-proliferative) and reduces appetite, also reduces inflammation (anti-inflammatory) and pain-relieving (analgesic). Common in Sour Diesel, Girl Scout Cookies, Headband, White Widow, Death Star, Candyland, Sherbert, and Thin Mint GSC.
  • Delta 3 Carene: Helps heal bones and boosts brain function, also reduces inflammation (anti-inflammatory) and dries excess moisture. Found in Arjan’s Ultra Haze, Bubba Kush, Amnesia Haze, Super Silver Haze, Lemon Haze, Super Lemon Haze, Cherry Pie, and AK-47.
  • Camphene: Fights cell damage (antioxidant) and lowers cholesterol, also reduces inflammation (anti-inflammatory) and clears airways (expectorant). Present in Ghost OG, Strawberry Banana, Mendocino Purps, Strawberry Cough, ACDC, Cannatonic, Banana Kush, and White Widow.
  • Borneol: Repels insects and fights cancer cells, also pain-relieving (analgesic) and reduces inflammation (anti-inflammatory). Found in Kali Mist, Hindu Kush, Golden Pineapple, K13 Haze, Diamond Girl, Snow White, Amnesia Haze, and Super Silver Haze.
  • Terpineol: Relaxing and calming, also lifts mood (anti-depressant), reduces inflammation (anti-inflammatory), and fights bacteria (anti-microbial). Common in Jack Herer, White Widow, OG Kush, Amnesia Haze, Girl Scout Cookies, Fire OG, Blue Dream, and Pineapple Express.
  • Valencene: Reduces inflammation (anti-inflammatory) and allergies (anti-allergic), also lifts mood (anti-depressant). Found in Tangie, Agent Orange, Pineapple Express, Clementine, Lime Skunk, Lemon Meringue, Orange Bud, and Grapefruit.
