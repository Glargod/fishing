Background and Context

Cannabis terpenes are aromatic compounds found in the trichomes of the cannabis plant, contributing to its distinctive smell, taste, and potentially its therapeutic effects. Research suggests they may work synergistically with cannabinoids like THC and CBD, a phenomenon known as the entourage effect. This section elaborates on the terpenes, their effects, and strains, drawing from reliable sources.

Terpene Details

  • Myrcene: The most common terpene in cannabis, known for calming (sedative) and pain-relieving (analgesic) properties, also relaxing and reduces inflammation (anti-inflammatory). Found in strains like Mango Kush, Skunk XL, White Widow, and OG Kush.
  • Limonene: Recognized for reducing inflammation (anti-inflammatory) and fighting cell damage (antioxidant), also lifts mood (anti-depressant). Common in Sour Diesel, Super Lemon Haze, Durban Poison, and Jack Herer.
  • Linalool: Known for reducing inflammation (anti-inflammatory) and pain-relieving (analgesic) effects, also reduces anxiety (anxiolytic), calming (sedative), and fights bacteria (anti-microbial). Found in Amnesia Haze, Lavender, LA Confidential, and Pink Kush.
  • Caryophyllene: Notable for reducing inflammation (anti-inflammatory) and easing arthritis, also pain-relieving (analgesic) and reduces anxiety (anxiolytic). Found in Girl Scout Cookies, Sour Diesel, Super Silver Haze, and Zkittles.
  • Alpha-pinene and Beta-pinene: Both reduce inflammation (anti-inflammatory) and help breathing (bronchodilator); Alpha-pinene improves memory and fights bacteria (anti-microbial), while Beta-pinene lifts mood (anti-depressant) and fights bacteria (anti-microbial). Common in Jack Herer, Blue Dream, Dutch Treat, and Haze Berry or OG Kush.
  • Alpha-bisabolol: Improves absorption and provides pain-relieving (analgesic) effects, also reduces inflammation (anti-inflammatory) and fights bacteria (anti-microbial). Found in Harle-Tsu, Pink Kush, OG Shark, and ACDC.
  • Eucalyptol: Relieves pain and fights bacteria (anti-bacterial), also reduces inflammation (anti-inflammatory) and clears airways (expectorant). Present in Super Lemon Haze, Girl Scout Cookies, Headband, and AC/DC.
  • Trans-nerolidol: Fights parasites (antiparasitic) and cell damage (antioxidant), also calming (sedative) and fights fungus (anti-fungal). Found in Sweet Skunk, Island Sweet Skunk, Skywalker OG, and Blue Dream.
  • Humulene: Slows cancer growth (anti-proliferative) and reduces appetite, also reduces inflammation (anti-inflammatory) and pain-relieving (analgesic). Common in Sour Diesel, Girl Scout Cookies, Headband, and White Widow.
  • Delta 3 Carene: Helps heal bones and boosts brain function, also reduces inflammation (anti-inflammatory) and dries excess moisture. Found in Arjan’s Ultra Haze, Bubba Kush, Amnesia Haze, and Super Silver Haze.
  • Camphene: Fights cell damage (antioxidant) and lowers cholesterol, also reduces inflammation (anti-inflammatory) and clears airways (expectorant). Present in Ghost OG, Strawberry Banana, Mendocino Purps, and Strawberry Cough.
  • Borneol: Repels insects and fights cancer cells, also pain-relieving (analgesic) and reduces inflammation (anti-inflammatory). Found in Kali Mist, Hindu Kush, Golden Pineapple, and K13 Haze.
  • Terpineol: Relaxing and calming, also lifts mood (anti-depressant), reduces inflammation (anti-inflammatory), and fights bacteria (anti-microbial). Common in Jack Herer, White Widow, OG Kush, and Amnesia Haze.
  • Valencene: Reduces inflammation (anti-inflammatory) and allergies (anti-allergic), also lifts mood (anti-depressant). Found in Tangie, Agent Orange, Pineapple Express, and Clementine.
